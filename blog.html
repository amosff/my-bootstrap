First Title

Loads within the power system are constantly changing. Each time a residential customer starts an electric clothes dryer or an industrial customer ignites a furnace, the power system load changes. This is why the task of matching generation to load is difficult, the target is always moving. An exact match between generation and load is only achieved for a short period of time. Loads change constantly, always creating another imbalance between the generation supply and the system load
